Welcome to Dog Adoption Agency, where we connect loving families with wonderful rescue dogs. Our mission is to provide safe, caring homes for dogs of all breeds and ages. With expert guidance and a transparent adoption process, we make finding your perfect companion easy and rewarding. Start your journey with us and change a life today!

About Us

Founded in 2016, Winnipeg Animal Shelter rescues, rehabilitates, and rehomes dogs of all ages and backgrounds. We run a network of foster homes, offer behavioral training, veterinary care, and post-adoption support. Our volunteers and partners prioritize transparent screening and thoughtful matching for lifelong, loving homes.

Why Adopt From Us ?

  • Comprehensive Veterinary Checks
  • Behavioral Training for All Dogs
  • Personalized Adoption Support
  • Lifetime Post-Adoption Assistance
  • Wide Variety of Breeds and Ages
  • Transparent Adoption Process
  • All Dogs are Vaccinated and Microchipped
  • Friendly and Knowledgeable Staff
